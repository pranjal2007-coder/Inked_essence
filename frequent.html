Inked Essence is a clothing brand inspired by creativity, individuality, and bold self-expression through fashion.

All our pieces are ethically made with premium materials, sourced and produced with care in the USA and select international partners.

Each product page includes a detailed size chart. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed fit.
